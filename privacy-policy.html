SoloBudget Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

SoloBudget ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SoloBudget mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SoloBudget:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Invoice data you upload (stored securely in Firebase)
  • Budget and expense data you enter

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To analyze invoices using the Anthropic AI API

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — for authentication and data storage
  • Anthropic — for AI-powered invoice analysis
  • Cloudflare — for API proxy services

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase and is only accessible to your account. We never sell your personal data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us at your support email.
